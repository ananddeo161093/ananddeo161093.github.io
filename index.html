Assistant Professor (Tenured)
Decision Sciences Area, IIM Bangalore

I am an Assistant Professor (with Tenure) in the Decision Sciences Area at the Indian Institute of Management Bangalore (IIMB). My academic journey began at Mumbai University with a B.E. in Electronics (2015), followed by an M.Sc. and Ph.D. in Quantitative Risk Management from the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research, Mumbai under the guidance of Sandeep Juneja. Prior to joining IIMB, I was as a Postdoctoral Researcher at the Singapore University of Technology and Design. My research focuses on analysis of high impact rare events in contexts in Operations Research and Quantitative Finance.

Core Research Themes

  • Quantitative Risk Management
  • Extreme Value Theory and Rare Event Simulation
  • Distributionally Robust Optimization and Chance Constriants
  • Financial Networks
